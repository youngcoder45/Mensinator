# Privacy Policy (Mensinator)

Effective date: 2026-03-25

Mensinator is a privacy-first period tracking app. Your data stays on your device.

## Summary

- Mensinator **does not collect, sell, or share** personal data.
- Mensinator **does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs**.
- Period and symptom data is stored **locally on your device**.

## Data we collect

Mensinator does not collect personal information and does not send your period/symptom data to our servers.

The app does not require an account, sign-in, or email.

## Data stored on your device

Mensinator stores the information you enter (such as cycle dates, symptoms, settings) on your device.

You can export/import data for backups or moving to another device. Exports are created on your device and are only shared if you choose to share the file yourself (for example, via a file manager).

## Permissions

Mensinator may request certain Android permissions for features:

- **Notifications**: used for reminders (if enabled).

If you deny a permission, related features may be limited.

## Third-party services

Mensinator does not integrate third-party analytics or advertising services.

Note: If you install the app through a third-party store (e.g., Google Play, F-Droid), that store may collect its own diagnostics/telemetry under its own privacy policy. This is separate from Mensinator.

## Data retention and deletion

Mensinator data remains on your device until you delete it.

You can remove your data by:

- Uninstalling the app, or
- Clearing the app’s storage via Android system settings.

## Children’s privacy

Mensinator is **NOT** designed to collect personal information from anyone, including children.

## Changes to this policy

If Mensinator’s privacy behavior changes, this policy will be updated.
